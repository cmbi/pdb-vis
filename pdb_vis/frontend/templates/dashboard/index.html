Visualisation

Many tools exist to calculate physicochemical properties of protein structures. pdb-vis brings several of those tools together and provides convenient visualisations of protein structure properties at the residue level, both for PDB and for fully optimised PDB_REDO entries.

2D

For each chain the following properties are calculated and mapped onto the sequence:

  • DSSP secondary structure converted to 4 states (β-strand, helix, turn, and loop). The state will be shown when the cursor is pointed at the color bars.
  • Residue molecular accessible surface area (Å2). The cursor tooltip will show the exact value.
  • Whether the residue is clearly solvent accessible (indicated by ^). A residue is defined as clearly exposed if it is more exposed than 10 Å2, or more exposed than a third of its accessibility in the unfolded state.
  • Whether the residue contacts symmetry-related residues (indicated by -).

3D

YASARA scenes show

  • The number of crystal contacts for residues in X-ray structures.
  • Metal ion centres and ligands. The rest of the macromolecule is hidden.
Download the freely available YASARA View to inspect scenes.
